Our Story

Growing More Than
Just Crops

Nestled in the heart of Chintakunta Village, Putlur Mandal, Anantapur District, Chandrafarms is a family-owned farm built on a single belief — that food grown with love and without chemicals is the best gift you can give your family.

For over a decade, we've been cultivating pomegranates, mosambi, groundnuts, and Bengal gram using traditional and sustainable farming methods. No pesticides. No artificial fertilizers. Just rich Andhra soil, clean water, and the dedication of real farmers.

Kummetha Chakradhar Reddy Owner & Farmer · Chintakunta Village, Anantapur
